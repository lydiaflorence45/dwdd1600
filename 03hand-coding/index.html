My goal is to share my love of reading by providing recommendations for some of my favorite books. The books I will suggest on this website are clean, easy reads in the middle grade and YA age ranges.

Don't know where to start?

  1. Pick a genre:
    Fantasy:
    Fantasy books feature magical or supernatural elements that do not exist in the real world and often take place on different worlds or in different time periods.
    Dystopian:
    Dystopian books explore a verson of the future where characters often face oppression, and usually end in mass rebellion
    Fairy tale retellings:
    As the name suggests, fairy tale retellings take classic stories and put a new spin on it
