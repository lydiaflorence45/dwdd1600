Keeper of the Lost Cities

Keeper of the Lost Cities is written by Shannon Messenger. Keeper of the Lost Cities follows 12-year-old Sophie Foster as she discovers a new, fantastical world, and learns to control her own fantastical abilities.

Keeper of the Lost Cities series:

  1. Keeper of the Lost Cities
  2. Exile
  3. Everblaze
  4. Neverseen
  5. Lodestar
  6. Nightfall
  7. Flashback
  8. Legacy
  9. Unlocked
  10. Stellerlune
  11. Unraveled (Coming December 2024!)

Check out Shannon Messenger's website!

The Traitor's Game

The Traitor's Game by Jennifer A. Nielsen is full of surprising twists and turns, love and betrayal. Join Kestra and Simon in their quest to destroy an imortal tyrant while unraveling the secrets of their pasts.

The Traitor's Game Series:

  1. The Traitor's Game
  2. The Deceiver's Heart
  3. The Warrior's Curse
