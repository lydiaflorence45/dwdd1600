Fantasy

A new and magical world

I think fantasy is my favorite genre! At least I know I've read more fantasy books that anything else. It is so fun to explore a new world. Characters with supernatural powers and magical beasts bring to life a story where anything is possible!

The Traitor's Game

Tress of the Emerald Sea

Tress of the Emerald Sea was one of four books that Brandon Sanderson wrote in secret during the COVID-19 pandemic. Tress of the Emerald Sea is about a girl named Tress who goes on an overseas adventure to save her best friend. Tress takes place in Brandon Sanderson's universe called the Cosmere, but that is a long story for another day...

Check out Brandon Sanderson's website!
