Fairy Tale Retellings

Classic stories with a new spin

I love fairy tales! These stories are so familiar so it is fun to see how these authors come up with ideas to make their take on a classic story stand out. Fairy tale retellings often center around finding true love and making dreams come true.

Entwined

Entwined is a Twelve Dancing Princesses story written by Heather Dixon. Entwined follows Azelea and her eleven sisters as they battle ancient magic to save their kingdom.

The Princesses of Westfalin

This trilogy is written by Jessica Day George. Twelve sisters uncover secrets about their kingdom and the realm beneath it in this exciting trilogy.

  1. Princess of the Midnight Ball (a Twelve Dancing Princesses retelling)
  2. Princess of Glass (a Cinderella Retelling)
  3. Princess of the Silver Woods (a Little Red Riding Hood retelling)

Learn more about Jessica Day George's fantasy series!

Check out Jessica Day George's website!

The Four Kingdoms and Beyond

Written by Melanie Cellier the Four Kingdoms can be read as stand alones, but for greatest enjoyment, should be read in order. Buckle up for a huge time commitment! Dive into the world of the Four Kingdoms in these adventures that span three series and one duology. These interconnected fairy tales each follow a young women who finds herself battling a darkness that has entered the land. Can she save her kingdom with the help of a dashing young man, and maybe find true love along the way?

  • The Four Kingdoms
    1. The Princess Companion: A Retelling of The Princess and the Pea
    2. The Princess Fugitive: A Reimagining of Little Red Riding Hood
    3. The Coronation Ball: A Four Kingdoms Cinderella Novelette
    4. Happily Ever Afters: A Reimagining of Snow White and Rose Red
    5. The Princess Pact: A Twist on Rumpelstiltskin
    6. A Midwinter's Wedding: A Retelling of The Frog Prince
    7. The Princess Game: A Reimagining of Sleeping Beauty
    8. The Princess Search: A Retelling of The Ugly Duckling

  • Beyond the Four Kingdoms
    1. A Dance of Silver and Shadow: A Retelling of The Twelve Dancing Princesses
    2. A Tale of Beauty and Beast: A Retelling of Beauty and the Beast
    3. A Crown of Snow and Ice: A Retelling of The Snow Queen
    4. A Dream of Ebony and White: A Retelling of Snow White
    5. A Captive of Wing and Feather: A Retelling of Swan Lake
    6. A Princess of Wind and Wave: A Retelling of the Little Mermaid

  • Return to the Four Kingdoms
    1. The Secret Princess: A Retelling of The Goose Girl
    2. The Mystery Princess: A Retelling of Cinderella
    3. The Desert Princess: A Retelling of Aladdin
    4. The Golden Princess: A Retelling of Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves
    5. The Rogue Princess: A Retelling of Puss in Boots
    6. The Abandoned Princess: A Retelling of Rapunzel

  • Four Kingdoms Duology
    1. To Ride the Wind: A Retelling of East of the Sun and West of the Moon
    2. To Steal the Sun: A Retelling of East of the Sun and West of the Moon

Check out Melanie Cellier's website!

Half Upon a Time

Although not strictly a fairy tale retelling series I believe it fits in this category. This fantasy series by James Riley takes you into a world of fairy tales. Join Jack, May, and Phillip as they fight evil in these twisted fairy tales!

  1. Half Upon a Time
  2. Twice Upon a Time
  3. Once Upon The End

Learn more about James Riley's fantasy series!

Check out James Riley's website!

The Lunar Chronicles

This Sci-Fi fariy tale series by Marissa Meyer follows four heroines and their adventures to uncover secrets, save their world, and find true love.

  1. Cinder
  2. Scarlet
  3. Cress
  4. Winter

Don't miss these other Lunar Chronicles stories!

  • Stars Above: A collection of short stories giving insight about both the past and the future of the Lunar Chronicles characters
  • Wires and Nerve: A graphic novel duology following our favorite android, Iko!

Check out Marissa Meyer's website!
